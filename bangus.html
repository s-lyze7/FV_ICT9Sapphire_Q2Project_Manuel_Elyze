Grilled Bangus Recipe
Ingredients
1 piece milkfish 1 lb. young tamarind
1 piece tomato (diced) 1 piece red onion (diced)
1 piece lemon 1 tablespoon ginger (minced)
2 teaspoons salt 1/2 teaspoon ground black peppe
Procedures
Wash the milk fish and dry it using a paper towel
Open the incision above or below the belly area of the fish and rub salt on the inside of the dish
In a large bowl, combine tomato, onion, ginger, lemon juice, and ground black pepper, then gently stir and mix
The mixture should be stuffed into the milkfish
Grill the fish on medium heat for 10 to 12 minutes per side
Serve with toyomansi and rice!
