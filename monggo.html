Monggo Recipe
Ingredients
1 cup mung beans 6 ounces pork (sliced into small pieces)
1 Knorr pork cube 1 bunch spinach
1 cup malunggay leaves 2 medium tomato (cubed)
1 medium onion (cubed) 1/2 cup chicharon (crumbled)
4 cloves garlic (chopped into small pieces 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 to 2 tablespoons fish sauce (patis) 6 cups water
3 tablespoons cooking oil
Procedures
Heat oil in a cooking pot
Saute garlic, onion, and tomato
Add the pork and cook until it turns light brown in color
Pour water into a pot and let it boil
Add the pork cube and stir
Cook mung beans on low to medium heat until tender, then add more water if needed
Place the malunggay leaves and spinach in a pot, stir, and cook for two minutes
Transfer the mixture to a serving bowl and sprinkle chicharon on top
