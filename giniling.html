Giniling Recipe
Ingredients
1 1/2 lb ground pork 1 1/2 cups potatoes diced
1 cup carrots diced 8 ounces tomato sauce
6 cloves garlic crushed 1 medium-sized onion minced
1 teaspoon granulated sugar 1 piece beef or pork cube
4 boiled eggs Salt and pepper to taste
3 tablespoons cooking oil 1 cup water
Procedures
Heat a cooking pot and add cooking oil
Heat oil, add garlic, and sauté until light brown in color
Sauté onions until they become soft
Place the ground pork in the pan and cook it for 5 minutes
Boil beef or pork cube, tomato sauce, and water, then simmer for 20 minutes
Add salt, ground black pepper, and sugar then stir
Put in the boiled eggs and turn off the heat
Transfer to a serving bowl and serve!
