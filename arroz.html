Arroz Caldo Recipe
Ingredients
1 ½ lbs chicken (cut it into serving) 1 ½ cups rice (uncooked)
34 ounces water (about 1 liter) 2 Tablespoons fish sauce
1 teaspoon garlic 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 cup onion (minced) 4 eggs (hard boiled)
1 cup scallions (green onions, minced) 2 knobs ginger (julienned)
3 tbsp safflower 1 chicken cube
1 lemon or 4 pieces calamansi 2 Tablespoons cooking oil
Procedures
Get a pot, heat the cooking oil then saute the garlic, onion, and ginger
Put some ground black pepper
Add the chicken cube and cook until it melts
Put the chicken and cook until it becomes brown
Add the fish sauce and uncooked rice then mix and cook for a few minutes
Stir and simmer the rice until the rice entirely cooks (30-40 minutes)
Add the hard boiled eggs
Add the safflower for additional color and aroma
Now serve it with garlic, minced green onion, and calamansi!
