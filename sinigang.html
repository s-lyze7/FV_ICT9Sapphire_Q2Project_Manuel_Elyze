Sinigang Recipe
Ingredients
2 lbs. pork belly 1 lb. young tamarind
1 bunch water spinach 2 Tablespoons fish sauce
8 pieces string beans (cut it into 2-inch pieces) 2 pieces eggplants (sliced)
1 piece daikon radish 8 pieces okras
2 pieces tomatoes 2 pieces long green pepper
1 piece onion 2 quarts water
Fish sauce and ground black pepper
Procedures
Boil young tamarind in 2 quarts of water for 40 minutes, filter broth with a sieve or strainer, and squeeze to extract juices
Boil tamarind broth in a pot, then add onion, pork belly, and half the tomatoes
Remove floating scums, add 1 tablespoon of fish sauce, cover, and simmer for 1 hour
Add the eggplants and daikon radish to the pan and cook for 5 minutes
Cook the long green pepper, string beans, remaining tomatoes, and okra for 3 minutes
Cook water spinach stalks with fish sauce and ground black pepper, then add chopped water and cook for 2 minutes
Place spinach leaves in water, cover, and turn off heat. Allow residual heat to cook for 3 minutes before serving
