Grilled Liempo Recipe
Ingredients
2 lbs. pork belly 1/2 cup soy sauce
3 to 4 pieces of calamansi 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
1 teaspoon salt Crushed 4 cloves of garlic
1/4 cup banana catsup 1 tbsp cooking oil
Procedures
Mix pork belly with soy sauce, lemon, salt, ground black pepper, and garlic, then marinate for at least 3 hours
Pour the pork belly marinade into a bowl, add banana catsup and cooking oil, and stir well to create the basting sauce
Grill the pork belly while basting the top part after flipping it over
Serve hot with spiced vinegar or toyomansi
