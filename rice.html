Egg Fried Rice
Ingredients
4 eggs 4 cups leftove rice
1 onion chopped 1/2 bell pepper chopped
3 sweet peppers chopped 12 tablespoons soy sauce
1 teaspoon sesame oil 3/4 cup green onion chopped
1/4 teaspoon salt 4 tablespoon cooking oil
Procedures
Crack the eggs and pour it to a bowl
Heat oil in a wok, pour eggs into the pan, cook until brown and crispy on one side, then flip and cook on the other side
Saut√© peppers and onion for 1 minute, then break apart the eggs
Add half of the leftover rice and stir fry for two minutes
Add the remaining rice and continue cooking until all ingredients are blended
Pour sesame oil and soy sauce into a pan and stir fry for two minutes
Season the mixture, then add the green onions and toss until well blended
Serve!
